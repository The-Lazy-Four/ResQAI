Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026
We value user safety and data privacy. ResQAI collects only essential operational data such as location (for emergency routing), system interaction logs, and user-provided inputs during crisis situations.
No sensitive personal data is sold or shared with third parties. All AI responses are processed securely via backend APIs. Local fallback systems may temporarily store minimal data for system continuity.
By using ResQAI, users consent to real-time processing of emergency-related inputs to ensure rapid response and safety guidance.
